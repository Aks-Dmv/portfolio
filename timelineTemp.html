Timeline

Having a consistent growth rate is the key to having an adventurous life. However, our growth might not always be constant over long periods of time, so having a potent feedback loop is a good way of staying on track. Timelines are very useful in helping people know the rate at which previous milestones have been reached, so that they can adjust the speed with which they are going.
